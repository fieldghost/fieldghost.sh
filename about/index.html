About fieldghost

The Ghost

I am a ServiceNow Developer slowly transitioning into a Bash & Linux developer and engineer. I firmly believe in the power and philosophy of Open-source software, and my goal is to spread the word about Linux, Bash, and the fundamental joy of learning to code.

My Mission

This site is built to be a place to learn. Everything hosted here is completely free to consume, copy, and study.

My Principles

Freedom to learn

Knowledge should be accessible to everyone without barriers, paywalls, or restrictions.

Open Source

Code should be public, auditable, and collaborative to foster trust and innovation.

Non-profit

Driven by passion and community value, rather than financial gain or corporate metrics.

Charity

Leveraging technical skills to give back to the community and support meaningful causes.

Coding

The art of problem-solving and building the tools required for a better digital future.

Learning

A continuous, lifelong journey of curiosity, growth, and breaking things to see how they work.

Developer

Building resilient solutions and openly sharing that knowledge with the ecosystem.

Linux

Embracing the philosophy of modularity, absolute freedom, and complete user control.
